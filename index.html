DarkSky NY

DarkSky NY is a chapter of DarkSky International, dedicated to preserving the beauty of the night sky by reducing light pollution in New York State. Our mission is to raise awareness of the importance of dark skies for both human health and wildlife, and to advocate for policies that promote responsible outdoor lighting practices.
